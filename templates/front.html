Autism in Adults

Common signs of autism in adults include finding it hard to understand what others are thinking or feeling getting very anxious about social situations and so on. Autism can sometimes be different in women and men. For example, autistic women may be quieter, may hide their feelings and may appear to cope better with social situations. Get yourself tested using this detector, and likewise take medical action wisely.
